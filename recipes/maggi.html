Maggi

Maggi is widely eaten throughout South Asia and can be prepared under 2 minutes

Ingredients

  • Maggi(Nestle)
  • Water
  • Some veggies like carrot,beans etc(optional)

Steps

  1. Take out the maggi and water in a pan and put them on mild stove
  2. Wait till the noodles softens then add the packet of spices that comes with the packet
  3. Add veggies
  4. Let it boil for 3 minutes and your maggi is ready
